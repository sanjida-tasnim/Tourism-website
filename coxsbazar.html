Cox's bazar is the most attractive tourist place of bangladesh and it is also the longest sea beach in the world.The main reason for cox's bazar being the most attractive place in bangladesh is because it is good for bathing,swimming, and sun-bathing and the sea is also shark-free.Cox's bazar sea beach is crowded almost through out the year.Anyone can go there both by air and road from Dhaka and Chittagong.

Places To Visit

Cox's Bazar, mostly famous for its beautiful sea beach and the sunset, has several attractions, including:

AGGMEDA KHAYANG:it is a large Buddhist monastery, and a place revered by around 400,000 Buddhist people of Cox's Bazar Hill Tracts.

HIMCHARI: it is located about 8 km south of Cox's Bazar, is a picnic spot famous for its waterfalls and hill top from where a splendid view of the sea can be seen. Birds and animals roam near the road.

INANI BEACH: it has interesting rocks and coral boulders. It is 32 km from the main beach.

COX'S BAZAR CIRCUIT HOUSE: it is located 2 km from Laboni Point, has a good viewpoint. The ocean can be seen from a height, and one can also enjoy the view of Cox's Bazar city.

ST. MARTINS ISLAND: it is the only coral island in Bangladesh, is situated at 9 km south of Teknaf. It is a popular tourist spot.

DULHAZRA SAFARI PARK: it is a safari park in Bangladesh which is situated at Chakaria Upazila. It has a wide range of exotic animals such as birds, snakes, and water beasts. It is one of the most affluent safari parks of the world.

Climate

Cox's Bazar is a city, fishing port, tourism centre and district headquarters in southeastern Bangladesh. The beach in Cox's Bazar is sandy and has a gentle slope; with an unbroken length of 120 km . It is located 150 km south of the divisional headquarter of Chittagong. Cox's Bazar is also known by the name Panowa, which translates literally as "yellow flower". Another old name was "Palongkee"

The climate of Bangladesh is mostly determined by its location in the tropical monsoon region: high temperature, heavy rainfall, generally excessive humidity, and distinct seasonal variations.The climate of Cox's bazar is mostly similar to the rest of the country.
