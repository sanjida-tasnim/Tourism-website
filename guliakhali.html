GULIAKHALI SEA BEACH

From artistic and structural aspects it is completely different from other beach. On one side of the horizon, the water is seen on the other side, while the Keora forest is seen on the other. Canow tree breathing around the canal flowing through the Keora Forest. This forest has gone deeper to the sea. Its environment is like the Swamp Forest and the mangrove forest. The green carpet of wide grass across the beach has made it bigger than other beaches. The narrow groove has been pierced through the middle of the green. The streams are full of water during tide. The unique position of birds, waves and wind mates is seen on this beach.

This beach is very beautiful.Every year many of people come here and see the beautiful beach.It is truly overwarlming to see the green with the blue.

Location:

The sea beach situated at Muradpur, Sitakunda in Chittagong Division. It is located 5 kilometres away from sitakunda bazar.This beach is at sitakunda district, not very far from the sitakunda bazar.The green watery field is very famous for this reason is that it looks like green bed in front of the sea.

How To Go:

Dhaka and Chittagong are linked by road ,railway and airway.Anyone can take a bus/train from dhaka to reach the chittagong city , and then again take a bus to go to sitakunda.There are direct bus and trains from dhaka to chittagong.If anyone doesn't like the road then they can come to sitakunda by airway.There is a very big airport called Shah Amanat International Airport , which is located at potenga,chittagong.This airport is very near to the potenga sea beach.One can come to potenga, then go to sitakunda by bus.But this is very time consuming.Its easy to go to sitakunda by bus and there are al so direct bus from dhaka.This buses can drop you where you wanted.

What To Eat:

There are no foodstalls.For which people have to take food with them.They can carry some snackes or pack lunch for them.They can also take packet biriyani or any other heavy food for lunch.water is also not available.So one should take water bottles with them too.
